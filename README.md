# About Me

With over 20 years of development experience, I’ve mastered an extensive range of programming languages, tools, and frameworks. My journey began with a passion for understanding how systems work, reverse-engineering code, and creating innovative solutions that push boundaries. Today, I’m fluent in nearly every programming language and capable of adapting to new challenges and environments with ease.

## What I Do

I specialize in:
- **System Design & Architecture**: Crafting scalable, efficient, and maintainable systems.
- **Full-Stack Development**: Building seamless user experiences and powerful backend systems.
- **Automation & Optimization**: Streamlining processes to enhance performance and reliability.
- **Security Analysis**: Fortifying applications to protect against vulnerabilities and threats.

## Why Work With Me

- **Innovative Problem Solver**: I excel at turning abstract ideas into actionable solutions.
- **Polyglot Developer**: Fluency in most programming languages ensures I’m effective with any tech stack.
- **Driven by Results**: My focus is on delivering impactful solutions that drive success.
- **Collaborative and Adaptable**: I thrive in team environments, fostering productive workflows.

## Let’s Connect!

I’m always open to collaborating on exciting projects or discussing new opportunities. Feel free to [reach out](mailto:j@jweb.io)!
